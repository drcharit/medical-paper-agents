## Prohibited Framings

<!-- Framings that are NOT ALLOWED in this manuscript. Enforced by Agent 14 (Scoring, soft metric S1). -->

<!-- For ALL papers: -->
- Do not start any sentence with "In recent years" or "Recently"
- Do not use "remains unclear" without specifying WHAT remains unclear
- Do not use "further research is needed" as the final sentence
- Do not editorialize results with adjectives ("remarkably," "impressively," "strikingly")

<!-- For NULL results (if classification = NULL), add these: -->
- Do not use "trend towards significance" or "trend towards benefit"
- Do not lead Discussion with a positive secondary outcome
- Do not use "numerically lower but not statistically significant"
- Do not claim the study was underpowered unless this is objectively true per the power calculation
- Do not use "further research is needed" as the conclusion (state what the null finding means NOW)
